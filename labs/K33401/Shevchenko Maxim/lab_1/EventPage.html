    Event infomation

    You can attend a professional workshop on chocolate, chocolate paintings or sweets. The master chocolatier will help you create masterpieces and tell you everything about chocolate - its history, interesting facts. Together with a chocolatier, we will cast chocolate in different shapes and decorate it with magnificent berries, various nuts, funny figures and fruits! Everything that will be done by you at the master class is yours. The master class is completely practical, i.e. Participants do everything by hand.
    The interactive master class lasts 60-90 minutes and will be a bright and memorable event for all participants.
    The most important thing in our master classes is that you are not just a guest, but an active participant in the production process. In the chocolate house, you yourself create chocolate masterpieces, completely immersing yourself in the creative chocolate atmosphere.
