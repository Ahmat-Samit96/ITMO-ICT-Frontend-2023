Description
Premiere of a dance mystery by Mikhail Venshchikov to music by Carl Orff

The ballet by choreographer Mikhail Venshchikov is a series of colorful and romantic images of the Middle Ages and the Renaissance. Love, knightly courage, Christian humility united in the dance. Together they remind the viewer of the issues of being, the most important for a person in any country and in any era. When creating the ballet, the choreographer was inspired by the philosophy and aesthetics of the Renaissance with its uncompromising faith in human capabilities. In this performance, the artists face difficult tasks - both choreographic and acting. The dancers transform into characters in the paintings of the great artists of the Renaissance, perform in duets, trios, sextets, and large ensembles. The culmination of the performance will be the story of the Knight, as if descended from Durer's engraving "Knight, Death and the Devil".
